Display PnL metric cards (Unrealised return, Cost basis) on the Analytics screen for the Wallet 4.0 release. Pressing the Unrealised card opens the PnL detail drawer (Unrealised / Realised / Total return); pressing the Cost basis card opens an info drawer. Portfolio data is sourced from `@ledgerhq/wallet-pnl`, visibility is gated by the `pnl` feature param and values respect discreet mode.
